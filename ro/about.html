Viziunea noastră

A clearer standard for luxury living.

Landmark Realty aspires to become the region's most trusted developer of heritage-quality residences — homes that age into their value, communities that deepen with time, and a standard of craftsmanship worthy of being inherited.

Misiunea noastră

Through slow architecture, and careful land.

By pioneering restrained, enduring residential projects — and by insisting on the long form of design, material and landscape — we work to challenge the assumption that luxury must shout. Our ambition is to enrich the communities we build, and the lives held within them.

Pilonii brandului

Clădit pe trei key pillars.

01

Măiestrie

Attention to detail is not a feature — it's the foundation. We pay attention to every element, from the quality of the stone and timber to the hinges, floor tiles, fittings and the proportions of a doorway. Craft is what lasts when trend fades.

02

Design atent

A home is not a structure — it is a carefully composed piece of everyday life. Our residences are generous in proportion, disciplined in layout, and reviewed through exacting design and engineering inspections before a single room is poured.

03

Calitate de autor

We are involved at every stage — from quarrying the stone and milling the timber to commissioning every fitting. Every residence passes a multi-tier quality inspection, and most of what goes into a Landmark home is made by Landmark.

Originea noastră

Patru parteneri.
One standard, from day one.

Landmark was founded in 2024, but the work started long before. The four of us spent our careers inside the industry — developing land, underwriting projects, running sites and placing homes. We built Landmark to bring those disciplines under one roof.

01 · Co-Founder · CEO · Commercial Development

Ali Ergun Ergen

Over 20 years of expertise in commercial real estate — development, leasing, marketing and property management. Starting in construction in 1995, Ergun worked on international projects in Turkey, Turkmenistan and Kazakhstan before moving to Romania in 1999, where he played a pivotal role in delivering Bucharest's first modern retail projects: București Mall, Plaza Romania and Băneasa Shopping City.

A specialist in retail site selection, property development, leasing and management — with additional expertise in land acquisition, governmental approvals and large-scale leisure projects. As CEO of Anchor Grup and Băneasa Developments, he oversaw €500 million in developments and managed 180,000 sqm of commercial real estate.

02 · Co-Founder · Residential Development & Construction

Robert Ion Erbașu

Over 25 years of experience in highly competitive industries — textiles and forestry — before transitioning into real estate development in 2019. Leveraging his entrepreneurial expertise, Robert successfully completed two mid-sized residential projects totalling 25,543 sqm and delivering 182 high-quality apartments — entirely funded with proprietary resources.

Brings a wealth of expertise in construction project management, cost control and site supervision. His hands-on approach and commitment to excellence position him as a trusted leader in residential real estate development, adept at navigating complex developments with precision and financial independence.
