The Address

An iconic silhouette above the forest.

Landmark 4 is the brand's most elevated work to date — and its fourth tower. Set on a private 1.8-hectare parcel along Drumul București – Târgoviște, the building rises as a single tapered volume, clad in hand-finished travertine and bronze-anodised fins, with a sculpted crown that reads as quietly from Piața Presei as it does from the Băneasa forest line.

Inside, one hundred and twenty-four residences are arranged across five typologies — studios, one-, two- and three-room homes, and four duplex penthouses crowning the building. Private service corridors that never cross residents' paths, oak joinery and nine-metre panoramic terraces are the standard, not the premium.
